## Logging In

1. Go to **`/login`**
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Login**
4. After success, you are redirected to **`/dashboard`**

---

## Dashboard Overview

The main dashboard (`/dashboard`) shows:

- **Greeting** — Personalized welcome message
- **Quick Links** — Cards linking to:
  - **Content** — Edit headlines, images, and copy
  - **CTAs** — Manage call-to-action buttons
  - **Testimonials** — Edit client quotes and photos
  - **Social & Contact** — Update social links and contact info
- **Recent Changes** — History of edits with date, user, and action

Click any card to go to that section.

## Content

**Path:** `/dashboard/content`

### What You Can Edit

- Hero headlines, subheadlines, and paragraphs
- Section text (Work With Brian, Attract Don’t Chase, Typical Outcomes, etc.)
- Background images and program card images
- FAQs, quote section, meta title/description
- Custom content keys (add your own)

### Steps to Edit

1. Find the field by section (e.g. Hero, Work With Brian)
2. Change the text or image URL
3. Click **Preview** to see changes before saving
4. Click **Save** to publish
5. Use **Undo** to revert the last save for that field
6. Use **Delete** (trash icon) to remove a custom key (the site will use the default)

### Add Custom Content

1. Click **Add new**
2. Enter **Key** (e.g. `custom.headline`)
3. Enter **Label** and **Value**
4. Select **Page** (home, keynote, attract-business, all)
5. Click **Add**

### Preview Before Save

- Click **Preview** next to a field or at the top
- A new tab opens with your edits visible (draft mode)
- Content is **not live** until you click **Save** (either on the dashboard or in the preview banner)

## CTAs (Call-to-Actions)

**Path:** `/dashboard/ctas`

### What You Can Manage

- Button text and URL
- Placement (hero, navbar, footer, floating, etc.)
- Page (home, keynote, attract-business, all)
- Active / inactive

### Add a CTA

1. Fill **Button Text** and **URL**
2. Choose **Placement** and **Page**
3. Check **Active** if the CTA should appear
4. Click **Preview** to test
5. Click **Add CTA**

### Edit a CTA

1. Click **Edit** on the row
2. Change fields
3. Edits are saved automatically when you move focus or click **Done**

### Delete a CTA

1. Click **Delete** on the row
2. Confirm when prompted

## Testimonials

**Path:** `/dashboard/testimonials`

### What You Can Manage

- Author name and title
- Header (quote highlight)
- Body (full quote)
- Image (URL or upload)

### Add a Testimonial

1. Click **Add testimonial**
2. Enter **Author**, **Title**, **Header**, **Body**
3. Add image:
   - Paste a **URL** in the Image field, or
   - Click **Upload from PC** and choose an image
4. Click **Add**

### Edit a Testimonial

1. Click **Edit** on the card
2. Change fields
3. Click **Save**
4. Use **Undo** to discard unsaved edits
5. Use **Delete** to remove (not available for default testimonials)

## Social & Contact

**Path:** `/dashboard/social`

### Built-in Fields

- **Facebook URL**, **Instagram URL**, **LinkedIn URL**, **YouTube URL**
- **Contact Email**, **Contact Phone**

Edit any field and click **Save** (or press Enter) to publish.

### Add a Custom Link

1. Click **Add**
2. Choose **Type:** **Social link** or **Contact**
3. **For Social link:**
   - Choose **Platform** (TikTok, Twitter/X, Pinterest, Threads, Snapchat, or Other)
   - The correct icon will appear in the footer
4. **For Contact:**
   - Enter **Label** (e.g. Website)
5. Enter **URL or value**
6. Click **Add**

### Remove a Custom Link

- Click the trash icon next to the field

## History of Changes

Located on the main dashboard (`/dashboard`) below the quick links.

### Viewing History

- Shows recent content, CTA, and testimonial edits
- Each entry shows: what changed, who changed it, and when

### Deleting History

1. Click **Edit**
2. **Option A:** Check individual items, then click **Delete selected**
3. **Option B:** Check **Select all (entire history)**, then click **Delete entire history**
4. Click **Done** to exit edit mode

## Preview

### How to Preview

1. On Content, CTAs, Testimonials, or Social, make edits
2. Click **Preview**
3. A new tab opens with your changes applied (draft mode)
4. The preview banner appears at the top

### Save from Preview

1. On the preview page, click **Save changes** in the amber banner
2. A success message appears
3. You are redirected to the Content page
4. **Note:** Only **content** (text and images) is saved from the preview. CTAs and testimonials must be saved from their own dashboard pages.

### Exit Preview

- Click **Exit preview** to remove the draft and reload the live site
- Click **Back to Content** to return to the dashboard without saving

### Preview Mode

- Preview is for **testing only** — not public
- Changes go live only when you click **Save** or **Save changes**

## Theme & Sign Out

### Theme

- Use the theme toggle in the sidebar to switch between **light** and **dark** mode

### Sign Out

- Click **Sign out** in the sidebar
- You are redirected to the login page

---

## Quick Tips

- **Save often** — Changes are not live until you save
- **Use Preview** — Check how edits look before publishing
- **Undo** — On the Content page, use Undo to revert the last save for a field
- **Session expired** — If you see “Session expired,” log in again
- **Toast messages** — Green = success, red = error
